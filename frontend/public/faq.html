Frequently Asked Questions

What is AirVnV?

The AirVnV is a web-based student accommodation and lodging system (STALS) application that helps users find and book suitable accommodation near UPLB. AirVnV allows users to search for various types of accommodation, such as transients, apartments, hotels, and dormitories, based on their preferences and budget. AirVnV also provides information about the facilities, amenities, location, and availability of each accommodation option. AirVnV aims to make the process of finding and securing student accommodation easier, faster, and more convenient.

How do I create an account and log in?

You can create an account by clicking on the "Sign up" button on the homepage and filling in required details. You can log in by clicking on the "Log in" button and entering your email and password.

How do I search for accommodation options?

You can use the search bar to find your destination. There are filters available such as type of accommodation, rating, preferred amenities, distance, etc. available to narrow your query.

How do I view an accommodation?

You can click on an accommodation card to see more details such as price, location, availability, etc. Browse on the most viewed accommodations or select a category to find the type of accommodation you are looking for.

How do I leave a review for an accommodation?

You can leave a review by selecting the accommodation you want to review. Scroll at the bottom of the page and click the "Add Review" button. Rate the accommodation based on your preferences. Additional comments are also encouraged to give more detail about your review.
